# RedShiftWyrm Site

Official website and canon archive for **RedShiftWyrm**, a dark fantasy worldbuilding project centered on Rhagnvyr, the humanoid form of the RedShiftWyrm, and the expansion of his Apostle, Fang, Harbinger, and domain hierarchy.

The site contains arc pages, character archive pages, lore entries, image galleries, and visual canon references for the RedShiftWyrm universe.

## Project Purpose

This repository is the public-facing website source for RedShiftWyrm.

It is used to organize and publish:

- Story arcs
- Character profiles
- Canon visual references
- Worldbuilding notes
- Image galleries
- RedShiftWyrm faction progression

The website is built as a static site using HTML, CSS, and local image assets.

## Canon Notes

### Rhagnvyr

Rhagnvyr is the humanoid form of the RedShiftWyrm.

Visual canon:

- No Apostle sleeve or contract tattoo
- Exactly two horns
- Anatomical right horn is smaller and electric blue
- Anatomical left horn is longer, dominant, and royal neon purple
- Viewer-side horn placement changes depending on camera perspective
- Core palette: obsidian black, royal neon purple, crimson red, electric blue, and small white/silver highlights

### Titania Veylorn

Titania Veylorn is the ancient Spirit-Fae sovereign of the Verdant Skychain.

Canon:

- Approximately 50,000 years old
- Former guardian queen of the Crownroot Confluence
- Becomes an Apostle of Rhagnvyr
- Ascends from six wings to eight wings
- Gains royal neon-purple slit pupils
- Bears the Apostle sleeve on her left arm
- Wields the Crownroot Graviton Scepter
- Departs with Rhagnvyr after appointing Aelyra Veylorn as Crown Regent

### Aelyra Veylorn

Aelyra Veylorn becomes Crown Regent of the Verdant Skychain after Titania’s departure.

Canon:

- Four wings
- No Apostle sleeve
- Regent, not Apostle
- Maintains visible rule of the Skychain after Titania joins Rhagnvyr

### Merfolk Bridge Arc

The Merfolk Bridge Arc follows the Vhalassar bloodline and Kaelithra Vesh.

Canon boundaries:

- Kaelithra is Harbinger-recognized
- Kaelithra is not currently an Apostle unless later canon changes
- The arc focuses on route access, bloodline pressure, Rhagnvyr’s entry, and the Skywell route
