# RedShiftWyrm Website

RedShiftWyrm is a dark mythic anime-fantasy world and visual identity project built around an apex cosmic dragon god, Apostles, hostile factions, enemy races, gravity pressure, redshift destruction, and cinematic fantasy worldbuilding.

This website is currently using a simple one-layer static structure for GitHub and Vercel deployment.

## Current File Structure

All files are stored in the root layer:

- `index.html`
- `README.md`
- `.png` image assets

No nested image folders are currently required.

## Nashara

Nashara is a cat-kin spy Apostle and envoy sent to investigate Blackforge dwarven technology, forge culture, rune-craft, defensive systems, political weaknesses, and how the dwarves survived the war after the massacre.

Her first contact with Blackforge Hold is hostile. The alliance is not in place yet. The dwarves distrust beastkin and elves, and Nashara must enter their world under suspicion, prejudice, and industrial gate authority.

Nashara’s visual identity is built around controlled seduction, cat-kin elegance, purple gravity pressure, black-crimson-gold luxury, and dangerous composure.

## Reilana

Reilana is a harpy commander tied to RedShiftWyrm’s eastern campaign.

Her battlefield role focuses on aerial pressure, command presence, harpy battle doctrine, and confrontation against siege-class enemies.

## Ogre Siege-Breakers

Ogre Siege-Breakers are enemy-only battlefield brutes.

They are muddy, asymmetrical, ugly, heavy, and designed for anti-air siege pressure. They use raw mass, thrown boulders, battlefield brutality, and overwhelming physical violence to test Reilana and the harpy forces.

They are not allies. They are enemy pressure made flesh.

## Blackforge Dwarves

The Blackforge dwarves are hostile-neutral industrial hold-dwellers.

They are not allies yet.

They are short, stocky, ugly-functional, forge-built, suspicious, culturally rigid, and deeply hostile toward beastkin and elves. Unlike ogres, they are not mindless monsters. They have culture, technology, discipline, hierarchy, prejudice, and negotiation logic.

They can be reasoned with, but only through leverage, proof, respect, and hard negotiation.

## Dromgar Ashthumb

Dromgar Ashthumb is an elderly Blackforge gate-warden and blacksmith patriarch.

He is Nashara’s first wall inside Blackforge territory.

He is old, ugly, broad, foul-mouthed, suspicious, culturally specific, and difficult to charm. He represents the Blackforge dwarf attitude: iron before silk, suspicion before diplomacy, and survival before courtesy.

His visual identity includes:

- old wrinkled face
- heavy ugly jaw
- grey beard
- massive blacksmith hands
- armored shoulders, wrists, shins, and belt
- heavy forge hammer
- gatekeeper posture
- blackened industrial armor
- forge-black, bronze, dirty gold, and muted topaz color logic

# Deployment Notes

This site is deployed through Vercel using GitHub.

Basic update flow:

1. Add new image files to the repo root.
2. Update `README.md` with asset notes.
3. Update `index.html` to visually show the new content.
4. Commit changes to GitHub.
5. Vercel redeploys the site.
