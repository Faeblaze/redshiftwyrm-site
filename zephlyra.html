Southern Arc / Storm Fang

Zephlyra

Zephlyra is the storm-born harpy force of the southern ocean cliffs. She enters the RedShiftWyrm saga as a savage rival to Reilana Sirocclume, challenging Apostle authority through speed, wings, talons, lightning, and territorial pride.

Her defeat does not erase her. It redirects her. Zephlyra becomes positioned as a future Fang of RedShiftWyrm, proof that the doctrine can spread through dominance, pressure, and transformation.

Lore Profile

Storm-Born Rival

Role in the Southern Arc

Zephlyra begins as a dominant cliff-born harpy warrior, shaped by ocean storms, height, speed, talon violence, and faction pride. She is not a passive recruit. She is a collision point.

Purpose

Her clash with Reilana proves that RedShiftWyrm’s influence is expanding beyond isolated Apostles. Reilana’s victory becomes a public demonstration of gravity pressure, hierarchy, and the right to command the south.

Status

Zephlyra starts as a rival power, is broken beneath Reilana’s Apostle authority, and becomes positioned as a future Fang. Her story expands the southern arc into a faction conflict, not a simple travel stop.
